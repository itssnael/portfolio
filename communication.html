BIC Friday

About the project

The goal of this project was to design a digital marketing campaign for the BIC brand during Black Friday. The main challenge was to ensure the visual was readable on a small mobile banner format while remaining adaptable to other media.

The Process

With my team, we chose a concept titled "BIC FRIDAY: Awaken your inner child" (Réveillez votre âme d’enfant). We aimed for a fun, colorful, playful, and educational universe that respects the brand's DNA, standing out from the typically dark Black Friday campaigns. We conducted a still life photoshoot featuring the brand's products. I was in charge of the multichannel strategy, selecting the distribution platforms, and creating a contest to encourage users to create and reawaken their inner child using BIC products.
