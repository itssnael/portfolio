OSINT - Web documentary

About the project

I participated in the creation of a web documentary on OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) and its impact on the fight against disinformation. Through videos, articles, and a website, the project demonstrates how OSINT helps journalists and citizens verify information they find on the internet and separate fact from fiction.

The Process

For this project, I created a motion design in After Effects to introduce OSINT. I started by researching this technology to structure the script and narrative flow of the video. Then, I created a storyboard to organize the different shots. Finally, I produced the video using After Effects.

Au-delà des silences

About the project

Originally a final-year project by master’s students at UVSQ, "Au-delà des silences" is a short documentary shedding light on the isolation of elderly people. Serving on the audiovisual team, my contributions focused on assistant directing and editing.

The Process

My responsibilities included preparing interviews and staging planned scenes, alongside coordinating the technical crews. Furthermore, the role involved sorting and organizing the raw footage to streamline the final editing phase.

EPK Jean-Marie Lavallé

About the project

Produced as an academic project, this Electronic Press Kit (EPK) features an interview with Jean-Marie Lavallé, composer and creator of the YIM machine.

Stepping into the shoes of a journalist, this shoot offered a fresh perspective by placing me in front of the camera rather than behind it. Experiencing audiovisual production from an interviewer's standpoint was both a challenging first and a highly rewarding experience.
